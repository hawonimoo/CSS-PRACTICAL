About Us

D.Tech Cohort 6 brings together ambitious students who are eager to learn, create, and innovate. Our mission is to empower young people with digital skills, promote teamwork, and provide opportunities for personal and professional development. We believe that technology can create positive change in our communities and beyond.

Our Vision

To become a leading community of young innovators who use technology to solve real-world challenges and create sustainable opportunities.
